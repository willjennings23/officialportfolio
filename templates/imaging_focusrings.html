001 : TK-9 Block 21 Focus Rings

I prototyped 6 iterations of focus rings in SolidWorks via 3D-printing for 25mm and 50mm RGB and MWIR cameras to improve both accuracy and precision, and to prevent lens extension into the payload glass. I put my rapid prototyping skills to use, and got to design production parts to be utilized in fire mapping applications.

SolidWorks assembly of imager focus rings and bracket

Problem Statement

The previous focus mechanism had teeth along the entire perimeter giving the lens its full range of motion. During flight, in the event of a software glitch or a poor initial focus, the focus motor could drive the lens in a full range of motion, extending the lens into the imaging window, potentially damaging the lens, or scratching the glass. The screws had also fallen out due to vibrations during flight, resulting in loose screws within the payload.

Objectives

- Design two-part focus rings that fit both the MWIR and RGB lenses, and can withstand vibrations during flight

- Restrict the range of motion of the lens such that it can't hit the imaging window but can focus properly at 36,000ft above ground level (AGL)

- Ensure that the focus motor spur gear could remain in contact with the focus ring teeth in the event of being over-driven

Actions

- Designed 6 prototypes of the rings in SolidWorks, and 3D-printed them in ABS plastic

- Used 2 set screws separated by 120 degrees to secure the ring to the lens, and 60 degrees of motion for focusing

- Performed testing with a focus motor and live camera feed to pinpoint the proper focal distance of the lens

- Used loc-tite to ensure the set screws are not susceptible to vibrations

- Tapped and helicoiled through holes to create strong threads for set screws

- Created CNC-machined focus motor mounts to attach to imager tray and drive focus rings

Results

- Two pairs of focus rings and stops added to production inventory and future payload builds for the TK-9 Block 21 and TK-8 payloads

- Eliminated risk of lens extension into glass, and loose screws

- Custom bracket to mount focus motors to central camera
