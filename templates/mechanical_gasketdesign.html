001 : Gasket Design

During my co-op at Overwatch Imaging, I designed a series of gaskets using my knowledge of test formulation, material selection, and sealing. The gaskets were implemented in to the PT-8 Block 10, Overwatch's highest-tech aerial imaging payload to be deployed in maritime environments. I developed relations with a gasket supplier, and implemented my gasket designs into the production supply chain.

Final production neoprene gaskets

Problem Statement

When flying at high-speeds in humid maritime environments such as Panama, a major issue occurred where water was entering into the payload, and humid air was resulting in condensation on camera lenses.

PT-8 after flight through precipitation

Objectives

- Develop a solution to eliminate water intrusion into the PT-8 Block 10 during flight

- Design for manufacturing, and incorporate into production supply chain

- Find the best supplier, and create a relationship with them

Actions

- Created a test rig that modeled the exterior mating surfaces to replicate the real gasket interface

Gasket test rig and inside lip of lid to replicate payload mating surface

10mm, 20mm, and 40mm hole spacing covers to determine optimal screw distance

- Ordered water-jet neoprene gaskets of various durometer ratings, thicknesses, and hole patterns from various suppliers to determine the most effective sealant

- Used water-intrusion paste inside the rig along with different torque ratings on the securing screws to locate gasket leakages

Spray test to emulate precipitation at high speeds during flight

Water detecting paste indicating a leak with the gasket prototype

- Designed 8 gaskets for each interface on the PT-8 Block 10 payload in SolidWorks

Results

- Installed the gaskets into the payload and confirmed their efficacy

- Eliminated water intrusion inside the payload, as well as significantly reduced humid air entering the system and condensing on critical electronics

- Created a supplier contract with a gasket manufacturer and implemented them into production
