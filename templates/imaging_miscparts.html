004 : Miscellaneous Camera Parts

Over the course of my time at Overwatch Imaging, I had the opportunity to work on various camera parts to support the imagers in payloads. Here are some of the parts I designed using SLA and FDM 3D printing techniques.

>>scroll for more<<

Lens Hood

In order to prevent LED's reflecting on to the imaging window and back on to the lens, I designed a lens hood to secure to the lens and have clearance to not contact the germanium glass.

SolidWorks model of LWIR lens hood

Part in payload assembly

SLA prototype to check part fit

Optics Cover

To prevent any potential scratches to payload imaging windows, I designed an optics cover in SolidWorks to magnetically attach to the payload, and easily be removed prior to flight. It was printed in bright red SLS nylon to avoid forgetting to remove it.

Optics cover bottom to interface with imaging window magnets

Optics cover in use on TK-8 imaging payload
