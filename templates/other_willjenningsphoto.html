001 : willjenningsphoto

Outside of engineering, I'm an avid adventure photographer and I take every opportunity I get to throw myself at a new experience outside my comfort zone. From solo trips to Alaska, to hiking through the Central Oregon High Desert, adventure photography is my excuse for seeing what I'm capable of and documenting it.

Tongass National Rainforest, Douglas Island, Alaska

Smith Rock State Park, Central Oregon High Desert, Oregon

Half Dome, Yosemite National Park, California

With the goal of one day travelling solely off the income made from my photographs, I created an online print shop in 2020 showcasing my finest work. On the site, I sell canvases and multiple frame styles of over 70 fine art photographs, each with a custom written description for my target audience. In the process, I taught myself how to:

- Interface with customers

- Market and deliver high quality products

- Work with suppliers

- Solicit valuable user feedback
